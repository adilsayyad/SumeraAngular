Yatra Holidays FAQs
Q. Why book holidays with Yatra?
A: The pay INR 1000 and hold the holiday for 24 to 48 hours is one of the top reasons you should book your holiday with Yatra, outside of the very lucrative EMI option for making the payment. So, even if you are strapped for cash, you can still go ahead and book a lavish holiday. Another reason for booking your holiday packages with Yatra is its range of inventory which covers small and big destinations, tiny historical towns, grand beach resorts, sea-side hamlets, backwater retreats, Himalayan ski resorts, Ayurvedic getaways, pilgrimage cities, famous circuits, archaeological towns to jungle safari stays. You simply need to pick your category of holiday whether adventure-based, for honeymoon, for its history, wildlife, nature or a family getaway, and you will be shown expansive itineraries on the same, from which you can choose depending on your budget and interests. Yatra holiday packages clearly state the inclusions and exclusions of your tour, leaving no ambiguity in terms of planning your budget and other essentials. Not to mention, there is the possibility of customising the itinerary with help from Yatra’s Holiday experts. Another gigantic upside of booking a holiday with Yatra whether domestic or international is the attractive deals and offers one can avail with a booking, the opportunity to earn eCash, making your holiday value for money.

Q. What does Yatra Holidays offer?
A: Choose any of Yatra’s tour packages whether domestic or international, and enjoy a well tied-up itinerary which takes care of your accommodation, offering you the choice between 3 star to 5-star hotels, meals, an engaging itinerary which is all-encompassing and covers all major highlights of a destination, outside of offering travel utilities such as transfers to and from airport to the hotel, transfers during sightseeing, cruises, if any, and even flights. Your holiday begins once your step outside your home and land at the airport, from providing flight tickets, to hotel stays and entrance fee to monuments and other places of sightsee, a Yatra holiday itinerary is well-rounded. Depending on your preferences you can look up exclusive honeymoon packages, weekend getaway packages, family holiday packages, last-minute holiday deals, packages for adventure enthusiasts, solo travellers. Whether you seek an unparalleled experience in the dunes of Jaisalmer, the backwaters of Kumarakom, the meadows of Kashmir, or a tea estate in Assam, we have everything cut out specially for you.

Q. How to book Holidays with Yatra?
A: You can use the extremely savvy Yatra app and even the website to make your Holiday booking at the click of a button. To book one of the travel packages you need to go to the Holiday section and set the filters to the city you wish to visit alongside entering your dates of travel, the duration and search holidays. Go through the various packages to your desired destination and pick the one which meets your criteria in terms of number of days, the places listed in the itinerary, the sightseeing it involves, and more importantly, the tariff. Once you have zeroed in on a package, you have the freedom to choose a hotel of your choice and customise the itinerary as per your tastes. You then have the choice of booking the package directly or sending a query to our travel experts who would offer you immediate support and help you book your holiday
